# Skella & Co

**Strategy for agentic systems and emerging technology.**

Code and design are becoming machine work. The harder question is which systems to deploy, what they should optimise for, and who holds the wheel when they run. Skella & Co helps organisations answer that.

We design the strategy behind agentic systems — the intent, the guardrails, the measurement — then stay close enough to operate them. Our wider brief is the same one we've had for twenty years: read what's emerging, treat change as opportunity, and help leaders make clearer decisions about what to build next.

## What we do

- **Agentic system strategy.** Deciding which workflows to hand to AI agents, how they should be bounded, and what good looks like once they are running.
- **Emerging technology foresight.** Reading signals early, separating durable shifts from noise, and translating them into options a leadership team can actually act on.
- **Experience and product strategy.** Shaping the human side of systems that are increasingly run by software — the interfaces, the hand-offs, and the trust model.

## Who it is for

Businesses from startup through to blue chip. Clients tend to come to us when the technology roadmap has outpaced the strategy, or when the strategy was written before the current wave of AI arrived.
